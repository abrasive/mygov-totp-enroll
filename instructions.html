myGov TOTP enroller

This tool will get you a TOTP key (as a QR code) for logging in to myGov.

You then need to use your TOTP authenticator to log in, every time, unless you disable it after logging in.

After you log in to myGov, you will be shown the QR code. You need to enroll this in your authenticator app - or maybe more than one, if you want a backup. Then, enter the current generated code to activate it.

The TOTP access method will not be activated unless you enter a correct key. This also means you won't be locked out if something goes wrong in the process.

To get started, just click the button.
